Embrace the Silence

An Immersive Synesthetic Prelude at Nuanu

On June 7 & 8, in a sacred wooden circle, where forest meets breath,

we invite you to a moment of stillness

—before music, before meaning—

where silence gives birth to sound.

we open a quiet door…

into something deeper than sound,

more delicate than light.

You are invited to enter The Theory of Silence —

to be among the first,

not as an audience,

but as a participant in the birth of presence

One day.

Two acts.

Three hours.

You, in the center of it all.

You'll witness Acts 0 and 1 of The Theory of Silence:

3 hour immersive journey in sound, scent, and light.

Over the course of two hours, you'll be guided through the first acts of The Theory of Silence — anchoring and grounding — a sonic and sensory invocation of origin.

From the origins of matter — silence, tremor, vibration —

to the rise of rhythm, shimmer of melody, and ancestral music.

From formlessness to form..

From deep primordial rumbles, like stars forming in the dark,

to the gentle emergence of breath, rhythm, and life —

this is where the Earth begins to sing.

We begin with the silence before the stars.

Low rumbles rise like tectonic breath.

Cosmic rhythm emerges.

Then the first voices —

rooted in earth, wrapped in harmony —

sing us into form.

Not a party. Not a meditation.

It is an experience.

Sound will surround you.

Light will guide you.

Scent will breathe with you.

And silence… will reveal you.

Please arrive gently with an open heart and a still mind. Dress comfortably

Leave behind shoes, words, and screens.

No applause—only stillness, breath, and presence.

Speak only in whispers, and respect the sanctity of the shared silence.

This is an invitation-only preview,

Crafted for artists, engineers, healers, and visionaries, dreamers, and those who sense

that something sacred is stirring beneath the noise.

This is a curated experience for creative partners, media, sponsors, promoters and collaborators.

June 7–8 | 6pm | only 40 guests per session
